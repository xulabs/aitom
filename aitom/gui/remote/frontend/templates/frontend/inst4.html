{% load static %} AITom GUI Now, you can see the name of file that you have selected.
Enter the coordinates for zooming in the following order:
  1. Left Upper X
  2. Left Upper Y
  3. Left Upper Z
  4. Right Down X
  5. Right Down Y
  6. Right Down Z
Kindly take care that left-up point should be smaller than right-down point.

After you click on submit, some magic for extracting a new zoomed in model will occur, and you will be able to see the new model here :)

OR
Enter the coordinates for slicing in the following order:
  1. Centre X
  2. Centre Y
  3. Centre Z
  4. Rotation X
  5. Rotation Y
  6. Rotation Z


After you click on submit, some magic for extracting a slice of model will occur, and you will be able to see the slice here :)
